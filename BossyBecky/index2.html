Cooking By Becky


Cooking is a fundamental human necessity. As old as recorded history, and perhaps time itself, the need to prepare food due to a variety of diseases, protection from other insects, and taste has superseded many common everyday necessities, even to this day. Being able to prepare delicious meals created endorphins to flow freely as taste became more and more important to a culture’s identity and appealed to an individual’s psyche. With the modern advent of the Food and Drug Administration, or the FDA, many foods and ingredients of meals have become much safer to prepare and ingest, increasing the availability of rarer and most tasty ingredients! I love trying new things, to an extent, however more than ever before in the history of mankind, we can now experience tastes and flavors from many lands. Let’s dive, mouth first, into the vast abyss that is different countries cuisines, delicacies, and flavors. Thanks for joining us here at BossyBecky.com!
Let’s get cooking, folks!
- Bossy Becky
