Raspberry Sorbet!

    Author: Six Sisters Stuff

  • Recipe type: Dessert
  • Cuisine: American
  • Prep time: 20 mins
  • Total time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 6-8

This Raspberry Sorbet has a lot less calories than your average ice cream, but it still satisfies those sweet cravings!!

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup water, boiling
  • 0.3 oz package raspberry sugar-free jell-o
  • 6 oz raspberry-lemonade concentrate, frozen (I used half a 12 oz container)
  • 3 cups frozen raspberries
  • 8 oz fat-free cool whip

Instructions


In a large freezer-safe container, combine boiling water and jell-o. Whisk for about 1-2 minutes, until all jell-o has been dissolved. Add in frozen concentrate and mix well.
Refrigerate for 15 minutes, until the mixture has set up slightly. In a separate bowl, mash your frozen raspberries. Add the raspberries to the jell-o mixture, and then fold in the cool whip. Mix thoroughly.
Cover and freeze for 4-5 hours, until it has completely set up.

Blueberry Croissant Breakfast Bake

    Author: Six Sisters Stuff

  • Recipe type: Dessert
  • Cuisine: American
  • Prep time: 20 mins
  • Total time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 6-8

      • Ingredients

    • 6 large bakery croissants, preferably day old, cut into cubes
    • 8 eggs, beaten
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
    • 1 cup mashed bananas (about 2 large ripe bananas)
    • 1 cup milk
    • 1/4 cup brown sugar
    • 1 cup fresh blueberries
    • Instructions

    Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Spray a 9x13" baking pan with non-stick cooking spray. Cut croissants into bite-sized pieces and place in the prepared pan. In a large bowl, mix eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, bananas, and milk until completely incorporated. Pour over croissants and use a spatula to press down on the croissants, making sure that every piece is saturated in the liquid mixture. Sprinkle brown sugar and blueberries on top then bake for 30-35 minutes (or until the center is firm and fully cooked). Remove from oven and let sit for 5-10 minutes, then cut into pieces and top with whipped cream and fresh blueberries to serve. You can also drizzle maple syrup on top.
